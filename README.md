**Tydes: Ride the Tyde!** 

Aug 19 2024 Still developing...

Ever find yourself scrolling endlessly on TikTok or Instagram? Say goodbye to wasted hours with Tydes! Tydes is a fun and community-driven productivity app designed to help Gen Z reduce excessive screen time. Leveraging social interaction, gamified timers, and personalized challenges, Tydes transforms the digital detox experience into an engaging and rewarding journey. Users can track their progress, compete with friends, and enjoy a dynamic, supportive environment that promotes healthier digital habits. With Tydes, productivity becomes a shared adventure, making it easier than ever to stay focused and reclaim valuable time​.
